### What I work on

<div class="card-grid">

  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-tag">Circularity</div>
    <div class="card-title">Circular Textile Intelligence</div>
    <p>
      Designing AI methods to recognise, classify, and trace textiles across their full lifecycle
      to enable high-quality recycling, reuse, and circular business models.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-tag">Digital Twin</div>
    <div class="card-title">Digital Twins for Textile Systems</div>
    <p>
      Building virtual representations of textile products and production processes to simulate
      quality, resource use, and circular outcomes before physical manufacturing.
    </p>
  </div>

  <div class="card">
    <div class="card-tag">AI + Fashion</div>
    <div class="card-title">AI for Fashion &amp; Apparel</div>
    <p>
      Using computer vision and machine learning to link visual fashion data with
      sustainability signals, enabling better design and consumption decisions.
    </p>
  </div>
