## About me

My name is **Channabasappa Muttal**. I am a PhD student in **TextileAI (TexAI)** at the Department of Informatics,
University of Bor&aring;s, Bor&aring;s, Sweden.

My doctoral research focuses on designing and evaluating AI-driven systems for circular textile economies
and digital twin technologies for sustainable textile production. I am passionate about bridging the gap
between cutting-edge machine learning research and real-world sustainability challenges in the textile and
fashion industry.

Before joining the University of Bor&aring;s, I worked as an academic researcher in computer science and
engineering, with a focus on computer vision, deep learning, and AI for fashion and apparel applications.

---

## Research interests

- Circular economy and sustainable textile systems
- Digital twin technologies for textile products and processes
- Computer vision and deep learning for textile analysis
- Vision-language models for fashion and apparel
- AI-driven design and production support tools
- Data platforms and digital product passports for textiles
